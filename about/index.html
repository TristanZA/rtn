About Road Transport News

Tristan Wiggill February 5, 2021

Road Transport News is the place to get news about the road transport industry in South Africa.

The news website is updated regularly with stories about road transport, logistics, trucks and buses.

The fast, secure and mobile friendly website reports on general developments and happenings within the road transport sector.

We cover truck and bus launches in South Africa as well as the industries that support trucking and logistics such as tyres, trailers, telematics, batteries and more.

We also publish opinion pieces by thought leaders in the road transport sector.

The business is the brainchild of Tristan Wiggill, a highly experienced editor, journalist and publisher who has worked in the transport and logistics media industry since 2013. 

Please free to send us any related industry news and opinion to tristan@roadtransport.news for possible publication.

A number of advertising opportunities are also available. Please call 073 972 5086 for more information.
