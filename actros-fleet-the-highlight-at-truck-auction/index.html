Actros Fleet The Highlight At Truck Auction

Tristan Wiggill February 15, 2021 Truck Auctions

Park Village Auctions says it has secured a bevvy of quality, bank-repossessed rigid trucks, tractors, and trailers for their latest auction.

Stand-out lots for the day include a fleet of 2018 Mercedes Benz Actros 2645 truck tractors, a 2017 MAN TGM 25-280 rigid, a collection of 2017 GRW tanker trailers, and a Kubota M130 tractor.

“The banks have commissioned a great selection of vehicles that offer inviting savings to fleet and logistics managers across the board,” says PVA spokesperson, Natashia Goosen.

“This auction is not to be missed.”

A Scania G460, a MAN TGS 17-374, a UD Quon GW 26-490, an Iveco Stralis 480, a Volvo 420, a Freightliner Argosy and International Pro Sleeper make up the remaining truck tractor stock for the day.

Other rigid trucks to be sold include a Powerstar 4035 and a Powerstar 2628 tipper truck, a 2016 FAW 8-140FL and various other models from manufacturers such as Tata, Mercedes-Benz, Hino, Nissan, Mitsubishi, Isuzu and Toyota.

Various Paramount, Henred Fruehauf, Kearny’s, Roadhog, Afrit, SA Truck Bodies and Top Trailers will also be on the block.

Hyster forklifts, a Caterpillar 140k Grader and Caterpillar 226B Skidsteer will conclude the sales for the day.

“This will be a simulcast auction, which is when we stream the live auction to online participants who bid against people on the floor in real-time,” explains Goosen.

The auction will commence at 10:30 am on Thursday, 25 February. 

Viewing will be held the day before (Wednesday the 24th) at PVA’s truck yard at gate number 2 of the Nasrec Showgrounds. 

The Nasrec Showgrounds can be found at Number 1 Nasrec Road, Johannesburg.

Due to Covid-19 safety protocols, the number of people permitted on-site for viewing and attendance at the auction is limited and booking is essential. 

For more information, or to register for the auction, visit www.parkvillageauctions.co.za and use web reference 678.
